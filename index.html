Welcome to Ancent Five Star Island Resort

Set amidst the turquoise Indian Ocean in the Watamu National Park, the 7 Islands Resort is a fusion of Arabic, Swahili and Indian design, handmade furniture and intricate brass lanterns. 7 Islands Resort provides a dream spectacular view of the Love Islands and their wildlife. The Resort offers 84 comfortable rooms, a Spa, a Sea view Restaurant and the renowned Sea food Restaurant Papa Remo on the Beach, three bars and three swimming pool, including a kids pool. The hotel has a fully equipped conference room with a capacity of 200 people. Translation and secretarial services available on request

Accomodation

Anent Five star Island Resort offers five categories of accommodation to suit all tastes and demands. All rooms are fully air-conditioned.
